Abstract

This ontology puts together all the ontology modules and ODPs that form the PFEEPSA (Poultry Farm Energy Efficiency Prediction Semantic Assistant) Ontology.

The PFEEPSA ontology was developed in the context of the IoF2020 H2020 project, with the aim of capturing all the necessary knowledge to support data analysts in the development of a system that warns farmers about potential future situations which may be uncomfortable or even harmful for animals.
